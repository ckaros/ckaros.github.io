Rebecca and Constantine

...or Becca and Dean

Our story began on the third floor of 31 Caro Street, Dean's senior year off-campus college apartment. It was Christmas time, which means plenty of mistletoe to go around, but Dean had his eyes set on one present, in the form of Becca. Through the stressful months of senior year spring and navigating life-after-college, one thing was certain, we would be together.

Fast forward four years and Dean proposed to Becca during a game of pictionary. Dean being the spontaneous, thrill-seeking specimen he is, picked out and purchased a ring then proposed all in the same day. With no real plan, he walked into Becca's parents' house with the ring in his pocket and figured it out as he went. If you know anything about Becca and her family, it's that they don't mess around when it comes to gaming, the competitive drive is vivacious. So naturally, the night ends with a game of pictionary. It's the last play of the game, our team is going to win if Dean draws 'outer space' and we guess it before the other team; instead Dean goes for the life-win and draws proposal/marriage and turns around with that shiny ring. Becca, in the spirit of competition, doesn't realize she hadn't won the game with the correct guess of "MATRIMONY" like she thought, until Dean was kneeling in front of her with that beautiful new ring!

It was perfect! No plan needed! Sharing the exciting moment with family present made it extra special! And now we can't wait to share our special day with all of you!
