(directions)

To help avoid confusion, Holy Cross recommends guests enter via McKeon Road and park in the Loyola Staff-Facility Parking Lot (see map below):

Take exit 11 off of 290 East, from the middle lane off the exit ramp go through the light, under the overpass.

Take an immediate right on Riverside Road, before the next light.

Take a right onto McKeon Road, drive 0.6 miles.

Take 2nd right onto campus on Loyola Road (after football field and Kimball Road, before traffic light).

Bear left at fork, then turn left into Loyola parking lot.

Drop offs at the Chapel through the gates are more than welcome. There is also limited handicap parking directly in front of the chapel, if needed.
